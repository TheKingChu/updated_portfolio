About

Negative Effect is a VR horror game where you the player must survive the night. Monsters will hunt you as you try to collect notes. The only way to survive is by being brave and taking photos of the monsters.

Key Features:

  • Survival Horror Gameplay: Navigate through a dark, eerie environment where survival depends on your wits and courage
  • Photography Mechanic: Use your VR camera to take photos of the monsters. Capturing them on film is your only defense and the key to your survival.
